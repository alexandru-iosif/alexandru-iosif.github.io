Research Interests

  • Applied and Computational Algebra:
    • Applications of commutative algebra, algebraic geometry, and semialgebraic geometry to polynomial ODE systems.
    • Computation and topology of discriminants of semialgebraic systems and positive toric varieties.
    • Algebraic transformations of polynomial ODE systems and their formal algebraic invariants.
  • Mathematics Education:
    • Interaction between capitalism and mathematics education (post-modern mathematics curricula).
    • Mathematical formation of teachers.
