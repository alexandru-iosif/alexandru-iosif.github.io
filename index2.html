Research

Google Scholar ORCID Source Code CVN

Research Interests

  • Applied and Computational Algebra: Applications of commutative algebra, algebraic geometry, semialgebraic geometry, and their combinatorics to polynomial ODE systems. Computation and topology of discriminants of semialgebraic systems and in positive toric varieties. Algebraic transformations of polynomial ODE systems and their formal algebraic invariants.
  • Mathematics Education: Interaction between capitalism and mathematics education, especially in the period following the introduction of modern mathematics in primary and secondary curricula. Mathematical formation of teachers.
